Ingredients

Carrot Cake Ingredients

  • 2 cups (260g) all-purpose flour (leveled)
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda (leveled)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 ¼ cups (295ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (190g) lightly packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 3 cups (300g) grated peeled carrots (2)
  • 1 cup (120g) coarsely chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup (70g) raisins

Cream Cheese Frosting Ingredients

  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter softened
  • 8 oz cream cheese softened (brick-style)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
