Ingredients

Carrot Cake Ingredients

  • 2 cups (260g) all-purpose flour (leveled)
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda (leveled)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 ¼ cups (295ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (190g) lightly packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 3 cups (300g) grated peeled carrots (2)
  • 1 cup (120g) coarsely chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup (70g) raisins

Cream Cheese Frosting Ingredients

  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter softened
  • 8 oz cream cheese softened (brick-style)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups powdered sugar

Cake Recipe

  1. With the middle rack in place, preheat your oven to 350°F.
  2. Prepare two 9-inch cake pans by greasing and lining the base to your liking.
  3. In a bowl, mix together flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together oil, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract.
  5. Then add in the eggs one at a time and whisk in between each addition.
  6. Begin incorporating the dry mix into the wet mix in three batches. Stir until the two are well combined and the batter is smooth.
  7. Stir in the nuts, carrots, and raisins.
  8. Divide the batter among the two pans then bake for 35-45 minutes. A toothpick should come out clean and the top of the cake should be springy.
  9. Let the cake cool for 15 minutes before frosting.

Cream Cheese Recipe

  1. In a bowl, combine the butter and cream cheese and beat until creamy.
  2. Add in the vanilla extract and salt and stir until well combined.
  3. Gradually add in powdered sugar and mix until completely combined.
  4. Now you're ready to frost your cakes!
